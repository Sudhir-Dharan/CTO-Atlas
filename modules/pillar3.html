Digitisation of the Value Chain

CTOs must leverage data analytics to optimize operations, viewing the organization through the dual lenses of the Supply Chain (flow of materials) and the Value Chain (flow of value).

Supply Chain vs. Value Chain

Supply Chain Mgmt: Focuses on the efficient flow of materials and processes from input to output. Keyword: Efficiency.
Value Chain Mgmt: Focuses on adding value at each step to meet customer demand and eliminate waste. Keyword: Value.

The 4 Drivers of Supply Chain Performance

Information is the linchpin that enables the strategic balancing of Transportation, Inventory, and Facilities to optimize the trade-off between cost (efficiency) and speed (responsiveness).

Impact of Data Strategy & Analytics

Data is the "new oil," and companies like Amazon aggressively redesign business processes to collect it at every touchpoint, using those insights to optimize the entire value chain.

Amazon's Data-Centric Playbook

Amazon Dash: Physical buttons provide real-time data on consumption patterns.
Anticipatory Delivery: Predictive analytics ships items before you even order them.
Amazon Key: In-home delivery provides unstructured video data for process optimization.
Amazon Go: Cashier-less stores analyze customer movement to optimize layouts.
